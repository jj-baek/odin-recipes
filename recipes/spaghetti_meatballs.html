Spaghetti and Meatballs

A delicious meal for those who somehow manage to burn water

This recipe is quite simple. All you need is a bag of costco microwavable meatballs and whatever boxed spaghetti noodles and jar sauce.
Boil the noodles, microwave the meatballs, combine everything with the sauce, voila bitch bon ape tits you have the meal that I eat to stave off the feeling of impending doom.
Is it healthy, no. Is it a gourmet fucking meal, no. Is it cheap, fast, and easy while tasting pretty decent? Yeah, so shut the fuck up (jk).

Ingredients

  • Costco Microwavable Meatballs
  • Any boxed spaghetti noodles
  • Any jar spaghetti sauce
  • (Optional) parmesan cheese
  • (Optional) red pepper flakes

Instructions

  1. Boil the spaghetti for .0003 - .0005 fortnights, microwave meatballs
  2. Drain the water, add the jar sauce
  3. Depending on your sauce, you may want to season more at this point
  4. Add your meatballs
  5. Add pepper flakes/parmesan cheese optionally
